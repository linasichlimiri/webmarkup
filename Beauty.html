Beauty

"Proper hydration is the main ally of healthy skin, regardless of skin type. Choosing the right moisturizer can make a real difference to the texture and appearance of your skin." - Dr. Sturm

Moisturizers by Skin Type

  • Oily Skin: Choose a light, non-greasy moisturizer. Water-based creams are usually ideal.
  • Dry Skin: Look for creams rich in oils and butters, which offer deep nourishment.
  • Mixed Skin Type: Find lightweight formulas that hydrate without creating excessive oiliness.

Tips for Using Moisturizer:

  • Always use moisturizer after cleansing the face when the skin is clean and slightly damp.
  • Don't forget to use a moisturizer on the neck as well.
